<p><p>The motion sensor lights market encompasses three main types: battery-powered, solar-powered, and plug-in electric lights. Solar-powered lights harness sunlight, providing eco-friendly, low-maintenance options for outdoor use, especially in areas with ample sunlight. Plug-in electric lights require a traditional power source, offering consistent brightness and higher output, suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications where power access is available.</p></p>
