<p><ul><li>Single-blade beam shutter</li><li>Multiple-blade beam shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beam shutter market is categorized into single-blade and multiple-blade beam shutters. Single-blade beam shutters consist of one rotating or sliding element, providing quick on-off control for light beams in applications like photography and optical experiments. In contrast, multiple-blade beam shutters feature several blades that open or close in unison, allowing for precise control over light transmission and exposure duration, making them ideal for scientific research and industrial applications where variable light handling is crucial.</p></p>
