<p><p>Automated pick dispensers utilize various sorting systems to enhance efficiency in material handling. The Pusher Sorting System uses a mechanical pusher to direct goods onto designated paths. Carbel Sorting employs a carousel mechanism for organized item retrieval. Line Shaft Diverters utilize a continuous belt system to route items based on predetermined pathways, while the Swing Arm Diverter utilizes an arm that pivots to redirect products. Each system offers unique advantages for sorting and dispensing items in various operational settings.</p></p>
