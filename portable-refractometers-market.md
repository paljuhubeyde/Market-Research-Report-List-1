<p><p>The portable refractometer market comprises two main types: manual and automatic refractometers. Manual refractometers require users to manually adjust and read the scale for specific gravity or refractive index, making them cost-effective and simple. In contrast, automatic refractometers leverage advanced technology to provide precise readings with minimal user intervention, often incorporating digital displays and connectivity features. Both types cater to various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and chemistry, enhancing accuracy and efficiency in fluid analysis.</p></p>
