<p><strong>The Three-Dimensional Parking Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSH</li><li>PJS</li><li>PXD</li><li>PPY</li><li>PCS</li><li>PCX</li><li>PDX</li><li>PSX</li><li>PQX</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Three-Dimensional Parking markets categorize various parking strategies. </p><p>1. **PSH (Parking Space Holder)**: Owners rent spaces without active management.</p><p>2. **PJS (Parking Job Share)**: Users share spaces for flexibility and cost-saving.</p><p>3. **PXD (Parking Exchange)**: Open marketplace for trading parking rights.</p><p>4. **PPY (Parking Pay-as-You-go)**: Users pay for actual time used.</p><p>5. **PCS (Parking Community Shared)**: Community-based space sharing.</p><p>6. **PCX (Parking Commercial Exchange)**: Business-oriented parking trades.</p><p>7. **PDX (Parking Dynamic Exchange)**: Real-time space availability updates.</p><p>8. **PSX (Parking Subscription Extension)**: Long-term subscription models.</p><p>9. **PQX (Parking Queue Exchange)**: Systems managing space waitlists.</p></p>
