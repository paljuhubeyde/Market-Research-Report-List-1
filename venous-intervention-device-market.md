<p><p>The venous intervention device market encompasses various types of products designed to treat venous diseases. Vena cava filters are used to prevent pulmonary embolism by trapping blood clots. Thrombus aspiration systems assist in removing clots from veins. Radiofrequency ablation systems employ heat to occlude varicose veins, enhancing blood flow. Iliac vein stents are designed to maintain vessel patency in cases of obstruction, improving circulation. Together, these devices aim to address venous disorders and enhance patient outcomes.</p></p>
