<p><strong>The Dust Measuring Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scattered Light Dust Measuring Devices</li><li>Transmittance Dust Measuring Devices</li><li>Gravimetric Dust Measuring Devices</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dust Measuring Device market encompasses various technologies used to assess airborne particulate matter. Scattered Light Dust Measuring Devices utilize a light source to detect particles by measuring the intensity of light scattered by them. Transmittance Dust Measuring Devices assess the concentration of dust by measuring the reduction in light intensity as it passes through a sample. Gravimetric Dust Measuring Devices collect particles on a filter and weigh them to determine concentration. Other market types may include innovative methods or hybrid devices combining these technologies.</p></p>
