<p><p>The Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillator (TCXO) market includes two main types: PIN shape and SMD (Surface Mount Device) shape. PIN shape TCXOs typically feature leads that extend from the body, allowing for easy insertion into PCB sockets, ideal for applications requiring repositioning. SMD TCXOs are designed for direct mounting on circuit boards, offering a compact form factor and efficient space utilization, making them suitable for modern electronic devices, such as smartphones and IoT applications. Both types ensure stable frequency output across varying temperatures.</p></p>
