<p><p>The steel pipes and tubes market comprises two main types: seamless and welded tubes. Seamless tubes are manufactured without welding, offering superior strength, uniformity, and resistance to high pressure, making them ideal for critical applications like oil and gas. Welded tubes are made by rolling steel strips and welding the edges, providing cost-effectiveness and versatility for construction, automotive, and general-purpose use. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, balancing performance, durability, and economic considerations.</p></p>
