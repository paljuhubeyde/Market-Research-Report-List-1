<p><p>The Firefighter Helmets market is primarily divided into two types: Structural Firefighting Helmets and Jet-style Firefighting Helmets. Structural Firefighting Helmets are designed for interior firefighting, featuring a full-brim design for enhanced protection against heat, falling debris, and extreme conditions. They often include face shields and safety gear integrations. In contrast, Jet-style Firefighting Helmets are typically streamlined and lightweight, ideal for wildland firefighting and rescue operations, enhancing mobility while still offering essential head protection. Both serve specific functions based on firefighting scenarios.</p></p>
