<p><p>The active tuned mass dampers market consists of three main types: vertical, horizontal, and rotational. Vertical dampers are designed to minimize vibrations in structures like tall buildings by counteracting vertical oscillations. Horizontal dampers target lateral movements, effectively stabilizing structures against wind or seismic forces. Rotational dampers address twisting and rotational motions, enhancing the structural integrity of buildings and bridges. Each type serves distinct applications, providing tailored solutions to improve stability and reduce fatigue in engineering structures.</p></p>
