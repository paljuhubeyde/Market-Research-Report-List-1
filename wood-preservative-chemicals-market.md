<p><p>The wood preservative chemicals market includes water-based and oil-based preservatives. Oil-based preservatives, like creosote and pentachlorophenol, provide long-lasting protection for outdoor and industrial uses, such as utility poles and railroad ties. They penetrate deeply into wood but have higher environmental and health concerns. Both types aim to enhance wood durability and lifespan.</p></p>
