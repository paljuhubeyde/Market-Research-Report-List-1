<p><ul><li>Epoxy Based</li><li>Silicone Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Die Encapsulant market primarily comprises two types: Epoxy-based and Silicone-based encapsulants. Epoxy-based encapsulants are known for their excellent adhesion, mechanical strength, and thermal stability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, Silicone-based encapsulants offer greater flexibility, lower viscosity, and resilience against environmental factors, making them ideal for applications requiring thermal cycling and moisture resistance. Both types are crucial in protecting electronic components from external stresses and enhancing the longevity and reliability of devices.</p></p>
