<p><p>Foil stamping machines are categorized into three main types based on their configuration: </p><p>1. **Flat-flat Type**: This machine features flat plates for applying foil to flat surfaces, ideal for precise designs on thicker materials.</p><p>2. **Round-flat Type**: This variant combines a cylindrical drum with a flat surface, allowing for foil application on both flat and cylindrical objects, enhancing versatility.</p><p>3. **Round-round Type**: Designed primarily for cylindrical products, this type uses two rotating cylinders to efficiently apply foil, making it suitable for labeling and packaging applications.</p></p>
