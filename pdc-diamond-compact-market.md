<p><p>PDC Diamond Compact markets primarily consist of two types: HPHT (High-Pressure High-Temperature) and CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition). HPHT diamonds are created by mimicking the natural conditions under which diamonds form, producing robust and thermally stable materials ideal for drilling applications. In contrast, CVD diamonds are synthesized through a vapor process, offering a more versatile and environmentally friendly option suitable for a range of industrial uses. Both methods cater to diverse market needs, enhancing efficiency and performance in various applications.</p></p>
