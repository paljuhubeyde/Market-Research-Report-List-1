<p><p>The industrial hearables market includes two main types: Personal Sound Amplifiers (PSAPs) and Hearing Aids. PSAPs are consumer-friendly devices designed to amplify environmental sounds, catering to individuals with normal hearing experiencing specific situations like hunting or birdwatching. In contrast, Hearing Aids are medical devices prescribed for individuals with hearing loss, offering tailored amplification and sound processing based on audiometric evaluations. Both serve distinct purposes but emphasize improving sound accessibility and communication in various environments.</p></p>
