<p><p>The hybrid security paper market encompasses various types designed to enhance document protection. Substrates serve as the foundational paper materials with embedded security features. Watermarks are recognizable patterns embedded into the paper, adding an additional layer of authentication. Security threads are thin strips woven into the paper, often visible and detectable under light. Holograms are three-dimensional images that change appearance when viewed from different angles, providing a visual deterrent against counterfeiting. Together, these elements create robust security solutions.</p></p>
