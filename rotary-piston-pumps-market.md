<p><ul><li>Single-stage Rotary Piston Vacuum Pumps</li><li>Two-stage Rotary Piston Vacuum Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary piston pump market is categorized into single-stage and two-stage vacuum pumps. Single-stage rotary piston vacuum pumps operate with one rotating piston, providing moderate vacuum levels suitable for applications requiring low to medium vacuum. In contrast, two-stage rotary piston vacuum pumps utilize two pistons in sequence, enabling them to achieve higher vacuum levels and greater efficiency. This makes them ideal for industrial applications demanding consistent and deep vacuum conditions, such as in refrigeration, chemical processing, and laboratory use.</p></p>
