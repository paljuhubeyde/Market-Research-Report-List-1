<p><p>The plastic denture market is segmented into partial and full dentures. Partial dentures are designed to replace one or more missing teeth in a dental arch while retaining the natural remaining teeth, offering improved functionality and aesthetics. Full dentures, on the other hand, replace an entire arch of missing teeth and are used when all natural teeth have been removed. Both types are typically made from durable acrylic plastics and are essential for restoring oral health, function, and appearance.</p></p>
