<p><p>The stainless steel forgings market includes various types such as castings, hot/cold forged parts, and sintered parts. Castings involve molten stainless steel poured into molds, ideal for complex shapes. Hot/cold forged parts are shaped under high pressure, enhancing strength and durability, commonly used in automotive and aerospace. Sintered parts are created by compacting and heating powdered stainless steel, offering precision and cost-efficiency for intricate designs. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, balancing performance, complexity, and cost.</p></p>
