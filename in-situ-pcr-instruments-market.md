<p><p>The In Situ PCR instruments market can be categorized into two main types based on the probes used: DNA probes and RNA probes. DNA probe instruments are designed to target and amplify specific DNA sequences within tissue samples, enabling visualization of genetic material in its native context. Conversely, RNA probe instruments focus on the amplification of RNA, facilitating the study of gene expression and localization within cells. Both types are crucial for diagnostics and research in genomics and molecular biology.</p></p>
