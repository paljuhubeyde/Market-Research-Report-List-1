<p><strong>The Fluid-Applied House Wrap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Barrier</li><li>Water Resistive Barrier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fluid-Applied House Wrap market includes two key types: Air Barriers and Water Resistive Barriers. Air Barriers prevent air infiltration and exfiltration, enhancing energy efficiency by reducing heat loss and gaining. They also help control moisture buildup within wall assemblies. Water Resistive Barriers, on the other hand, protect structures from water intrusion, preventing mold growth and wood rot while allowing vapor to escape. Together, these systems optimize a building's performance by maintaining thermal comfort and structural integrity.</p></p>
