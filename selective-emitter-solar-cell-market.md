<p><p>The selective emitter solar cell market comprises three primary types: Masking and Etching, Self-Alignment, and Self-Doping. Masking and Etching involves precise patterning to optimize material use on the solar cell, enhancing efficiency. Self-Alignment utilizes advanced techniques to align layers accurately during fabrication, resulting in improved performance. Self-Doping incorporates specific dopant elements during cell production, enhancing the electrical characteristics by tuning the emitter's properties. Collectively, these methods aim to boost solar cell efficiency and reduce manufacturing costs.</p></p>
