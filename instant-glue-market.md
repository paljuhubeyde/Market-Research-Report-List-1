<p><strong>The Instant Glue Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cyanoacrylate Instant Glue</li><li>Epoxy-based Instant Glue</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Instant Glue market is primarily categorized into two types: Cyanoacrylate Instant Glue and Epoxy-based Instant Glue. Cyanoacrylate Instant Glue, commonly known as super glue, offers rapid adhesion suitable for non-porous surfaces, making it ideal for quick repairs and everyday household use. In contrast, Epoxy-based Instant Glue consists of a resin and hardener that create a stronger, more durable bond, suitable for heavier-duty applications and materials that require high resistance to heat, chemicals, and moisture.</p></p>
