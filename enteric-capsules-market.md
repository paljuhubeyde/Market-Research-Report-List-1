<p><p>The enteric capsules market is categorized into three primary types: Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose (HPMC), Gelatin, and Vegetable capsules. HPMC capsules are plant-based, offering a vegan-friendly option that dissolves in the small intestine. Gelatin capsules, derived from animal products, are the traditional choice, providing effective encapsulation for various formulations. Vegetable capsules, also plant-based, typically cater to health-conscious consumers and those with dietary restrictions. Each type serves different consumer needs and preferences in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical sectors.</p></p>
