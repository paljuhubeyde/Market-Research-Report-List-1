<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet (UV) Curing</li><li>Electron beam (EB) Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Energy Curable Resins market includes two primary types: Ultraviolet (UV) curing and Electron Beam (EB) curing. UV curing utilizes ultraviolet light to initiate polymerization, resulting in rapid drying and strong adhesion, commonly used in coatings and inks. EB curing, on the other hand, employs high-energy electron beams to achieve curing without requiring heat or solvents, providing advantages in cross-linking and efficiency, often used in industrial applications. Both methods are environmentally friendly and enhance production speed.</p></p>
