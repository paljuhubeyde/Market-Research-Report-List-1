<p><strong>The Silicon Steel Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel</li><li>Non-Grain Oriented Electrical Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Steel Sheets market comprises two main types: oriented electrical steel and non-grain-oriented electrical steel. Oriented electrical steel is designed for high-performance applications, featuring enhanced magnetic properties, making it ideal for transformers and electric motors. In contrast, non-grain-oriented electrical steel offers isotropic magnetic performance, suitable for various general applications, including generators and other electromagnetic devices. Both types are essential in the energy sector, supporting the efficiency of electrical equipment and renewable energy technologies.</p></p>
