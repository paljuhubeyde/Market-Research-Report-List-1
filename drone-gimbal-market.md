<p><p>The drone gimbal market is categorized into three types based on load capacity: lightweight gimbals, medium load gimbals, and heavy load gimbals. Lightweight gimbals are designed for small drones, primarily used for smartphones and compact cameras, providing stabilization for basic aerial photography. Medium load gimbals accommodate larger cameras, suitable for professional videography with enhanced stabilization. Heavy load gimbals are built for industrial drones, capable of carrying professional cinema cameras and advanced imaging equipment, ensuring stability during complex aerial maneuvers.</p></p>
