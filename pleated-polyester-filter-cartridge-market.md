<p><p>The Pleated Polyester Filter Cartridge market is segmented into various types based on the material composition. PP filters are known for their chemical resistance and affordability, making them popular for general filtration. PES filters offer high flow rates and thermal stability, suitable for pharmaceutical applications. PTFE filters are renowned for their hydrophobic properties, ideal for gas and aggressive liquid filtration. Nylon filters provide strong mechanical strength and are effective in retaining fine particles, commonly used in liquid filtration processes.</p></p>
