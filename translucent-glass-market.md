<p><p>The translucent glass market is primarily categorized into two manufacturing processes: float and rolled. Float glass production involves melting raw materials and forming a continuous sheet by floating it on molten tin, resulting in a smooth surface. In contrast, the rolled glass process involves heating and rolling molten glass into sheets, producing textured or patterned surfaces. Both methods cater to various applications, including architectural designs, automotive, and decorative glass, reflecting the growing demand for aesthetic and functional translucent materials.</p></p>
