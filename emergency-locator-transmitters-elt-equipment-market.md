<p><p>Emergency Locator Transmitters (ELTs) are critical safety devices used in aviation to enhance search and rescue operations. The market for ELTs is divided into two main types: Fixed ELTs and Portable ELTs. Fixed ELTs are permanently installed in an aircraft and automatically activate during a crash, transmitting distress signals. In contrast, Portable ELTs are handheld devices that can be carried by individuals, providing flexibility for use in various emergency situations. Both types play vital roles in ensuring timely response during emergencies.</p></p>
