<p><p>The automotive emissions ceramics market primarily comprises three types: honeycomb structures, Gasoline Particulate Filters (GPF), and Diesel Particulate Filters (DPF). Honeycomb ceramics facilitate efficient gas flow while providing a large surface area for catalytic reactions. GPFs target particulate matter in gasoline engines, enhancing air quality by capturing soot particles. DPFs serve diesel engines by trapping and oxidizing soot, reducing harmful emissions. Together, these components play a vital role in meeting stringent environmental regulations and improving vehicle performance.</p></p>
