<p><ul><li>Solvent Extraction Method</li><li>Cold Pressed Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The apricot oil market is primarily categorized into two extraction methods: solvent extraction and cold pressing. The solvent extraction method involves using chemical solvents to separate oil from apricot kernels, resulting in higher yields but potentially affecting oil purity. In contrast, the cold-pressed method involves mechanically pressing the kernels without heat, preserving essential nutrients and providing a higher-quality oil. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences, with cold-pressed oil typically preferred for its natural benefits and flavor.</p></p>
