<p><strong>The Computer Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Perforated</li><li>Perforated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer paper market is divided into two main types: non-perforated and perforated. Non-perforated computer paper is a continuous sheet without any gaps, ideal for bulk printing and high-volume tasks, providing a smooth, uninterrupted print experience. In contrast, perforated computer paper features pre-defined tear lines that allow for easy separation of individual sheets, making it suitable for applications like invoices or reports where individual pages need to be easily detached. Both types cater to specific printing needs in various industries.</p></p>
