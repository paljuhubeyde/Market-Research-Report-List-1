<p><p>The cryotherapy units market is divided into two primary types: whole body cryotherapy (WBC) and local cryotherapy. Whole body cryotherapy involves exposure to extremely cold temperatures in a chamber or sauna, promoting recovery, reducing inflammation, and enhancing overall wellness. In contrast, local cryotherapy targets specific areas of the body using localized cooling techniques to alleviate pain, reduce swelling, and treat injuries. Both methods are gaining popularity in sports medicine, wellness centers, and therapeutic practices, catering to diverse health needs.</p></p>
