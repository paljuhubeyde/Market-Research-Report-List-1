<p><strong>The Semiconductor Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wafer Semiconductor Inspection System</li><li>Mask Semiconductor Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Inspection System market is primarily categorized into two types: Wafer Semiconductor Inspection Systems and Mask Semiconductor Inspection Systems. Wafer Semiconductor Inspection Systems are designed to assess the quality and integrity of silicon wafers during semiconductor fabrication, ensuring defects are detected before further processing. Conversely, Mask Semiconductor Inspection Systems focus on inspecting photomasks used in photolithography, identifying flaws that could lead to defects in the final semiconductor products. Both systems are essential for maintaining high production yields and device performance in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
