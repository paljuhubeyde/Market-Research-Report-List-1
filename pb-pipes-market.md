<p><strong>The PB Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H)</li><li>Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PB Pipes market comprises two primary types: Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) and Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R). PB-H is characterized by its uniform molecular structure, offering excellent mechanical properties, chemical resistance, and thermal stability, making it suitable for various applications, particularly in plumbing and heating systems. On the other hand, PB-R features a random distribution of different polymer units, providing enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, which is beneficial for applications requiring greater adaptability, such as in flexible piping systems.</p></p>
