<p><strong>The Turbidity Curtains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Floating</li><li>Staked</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turbidity curtains market is divided primarily into two types: floating and staked turbidity curtains. Floating turbidity curtains are designed to float on the water's surface, effectively containing sediment and preventing its dispersion. They are often used in construction and dredging projects. Staked turbidity curtains, on the other hand, are anchored to the bottom with stakes, providing enhanced stability in turbulent waters. Both types are essential for sediment control and water quality management in various environmental applications.</p></p>
