<p><p>The colored stainless steel market primarily includes two types: spectral stainless steel and PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coated stainless steel. Spectral stainless steel features a vibrant, iridescent finish created through a unique chemical process, allowing for a wide spectrum of colors. PVD coated stainless steel, on the other hand, utilizes a vacuum process to deposit a thin film of color, enhancing durability and resistance while providing a lustrous appearance. Both types are popular in architectural, automotive, and decorative applications.</p></p>
