<p><p>The garden light market is primarily divided into two types: solar and electric. Solar garden lights harness energy from the sun, making them eco-friendly and low-maintenance, ideal for areas without electrical supply. They offer portability and ease of installation. In contrast, electric garden lights provide consistent and brighter illumination, often ideal for larger spaces and intricate designs. While they require a power source, they typically allow for advanced features like timers and dimmers, catering to diverse landscaping needs.</p></p>
