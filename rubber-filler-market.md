In contrast, inorganic fillers, sourced from minerals such as carbon black, silica, and clay, improve durability, heat resistance, and overall performance. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing the properties of rubber compounds for diverse applications, including automotive, industrial, and consumer goods, contributing to enhanced product performance and cost efficiency.</p></p>
