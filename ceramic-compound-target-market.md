<p><p>The ceramic compound target market encompasses various types, including oxide, silicide, carbide, and sulfide targets, each serving distinct applications in thin-film deposition processes. Oxide targets, composed of metal oxides, are widely used in electronics for dielectric layers. Silicide targets enable the creation of high-performance semiconductor components. Carbide targets are often utilized in hard coatings due to their exceptional hardness and wear resistance. Sulfide targets, while less common, find niche applications in optoelectronics and specialized coatings.</p></p>
