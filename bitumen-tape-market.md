<p><ul><li>Double Sided Bitumen Tape</li><li>Single Sided Bitumen Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bitumen tape market is divided into two primary types: double-sided bitumen tape and single-sided bitumen tape. Double-sided bitumen tape features adhesive on both sides, making it ideal for applications requiring strong bonding between two surfaces, such as sealing joints or roofing. In contrast, single-sided bitumen tape has adhesive on one side, primarily used for waterproofing and insulation purposes. Both types are essential for construction, automotive, and industrial applications, providing durability and effective sealing solutions.</p></p>
