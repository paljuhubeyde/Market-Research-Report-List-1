<p><p>The infrared motion sensor market comprises two primary types: Passive Infrared Sensors (PIR) and Imaging Infrared Sensors. PIR sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by objects, particularly humans, to sense motion without needing active light sources, making them ideal for security and energy-efficient applications. Imaging Infrared Sensors capture thermal images by detecting infrared radiation across a wider field, providing detailed visual data for surveillance and monitoring. Both technologies enhance safety and automation in various sectors, including residential, commercial, and industrial environments.</p></p>
