<p><p>The carbon fiber belt market is categorized into three main types: wax-based, mixed-based, and resin-based. Wax-based belts utilize a wax coating that enhances durability and resistance to environmental factors. Mixed-based belts combine wax and resin for improved performance, offering balance between flexibility and strength. Resin-based belts use epoxy or polyester resins, providing superior adhesion and mechanical properties. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to industries such as automotive, aerospace, and sports equipment, where strength and lightweight characteristics are essential.</p></p>
