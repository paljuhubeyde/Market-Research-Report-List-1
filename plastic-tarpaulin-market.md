<p><p>The plastic tarpaulin market primarily consists of two types: PVC tarpaulin and PE tarpaulin. PVC tarpaulin is known for its durability, water resistance, and UV stability, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications such as truck covers, tents, and industrial uses. In contrast, PE tarpaulin is lighter, more flexible, and cost-effective, commonly used for general purposes like ground covers, camping, and temporary shelters. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction, agriculture, and outdoor events, fulfilling various protective needs.</p></p>
