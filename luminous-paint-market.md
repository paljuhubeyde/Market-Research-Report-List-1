<p><strong>The Luminous Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Paint</li><li>Phosphorescent Paint</li><li>Radio Luminescent Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The luminous paint market comprises three main types: fluorescent paint, phosphorescent paint, and radio luminescent paint. Fluorescent paint absorbs UV light and emits visible light almost instantly, ideal for vibrant displays. Phosphorescent paint, on the other hand, stores energy and gradually releases it, glowing in the dark for extended periods. Radio luminescent paint incorporates radioactive materials, producing light without external energy sources but is subject to strict regulations. Each type serves unique applications across various industries, including safety, art, and automotive.</p></p>
