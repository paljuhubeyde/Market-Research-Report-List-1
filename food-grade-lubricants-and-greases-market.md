<p><strong>The Food Grade Lubricants and Greases Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>H1</li><li>H2</li><li>3H</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food grade lubricants and greases are classified into three categories: H1, H2, and 3H. H1 lubricants are food-safe for incidental contact with food during processing and are often used in areas where minimal exposure occurs. H2 lubricants are not intended for food contact and are used in equipment that does not come into direct contact with food. The 3H category includes lubricants specifically designed for use in food processing environments where there is potential for food contact, including direct application on food.</p></p>
